Sled Friends Rescue

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide visitors with a site to rescue sled dogs (i.e Huskies, Malamutes, and Samoyed dog breeds, either as a family pet or for sled teams.

Elements

  • The pages using individual JSON information are the Available Pets page and the helpful links page.
  • The page using the HTML5 video is the home page

Target Audience

The target audience are persons who either would like to adopt a sled dog from the rescue, or would like to donate to the rescue to aid in its funding, target age range is for users who are of age to adopt a pet.
