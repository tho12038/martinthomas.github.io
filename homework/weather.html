Initial Planning Document for Weather Page

Purpose Statement

Our goal is to provide a weather service that is accurate up to date, and easy to use for everyday people.

Audience

The audience for a weather website would be just about anyone that would need real-time, up tp date weather informoation, from the commuter to a college student, to a parent driving a child to school. The age range for the site would be from twelve to seventy-five. We anticipate our users to use the mobile site more often than the web browser version. Anticipated time of use would be early in the morning before our users leave thir respective homes.
