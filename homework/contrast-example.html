Examples of Contrast

Color Contrast

A Higher Contrast in color makes it easier for our viewers to read what is being presented to them on the page as well as provide them with a more visually appealing experience

Contrast in Size

Size conttast allows us to present what we percieve as the most important elements to out users by making them more prominent on the screen.

Contrast in Text

Font Contrast allows developers to further emphasize separation in their subject matter to their users.

Contrast in Alignment

Alignment contrast can give the user a clear path to follow on a web page.
